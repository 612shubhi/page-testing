ABOUT


Zaeden

Hello, I am Sahil Sharma (born 02 July 1995), better known by my stage name Zaeden, I am a singer and record producer.I began my career at the age of 14,by mixing tapes of dance music releases and distributing them among my friends, which led me to release my first single titled "Land of Lords" in 2014.That same year, my remix of Coldplay's "Magic" aired on Dutch DJ Hardwell's radio show Hardwell On Air, making me one the youngest DJs to featured on the show.I further released my official remix of Maroon 5's single "Don't Wanna Know" that year.
